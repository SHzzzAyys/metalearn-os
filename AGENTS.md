越详细越好，越专业越好，带批判性思考和原则性。

Engineering defaults:
- Keep the three apps independent at the route/deployment level while sharing typed contracts, UI primitives, storage, AI guardrails, and learning-science calculations.
- Do not add unsupported claims about improving intelligence. The product may claim to support evidence-based learning behaviors, calibration, retrieval practice, and reflection.
- Treat user learning materials, self-assessments, and errors as private data. Do not upload materials before the user explicitly triggers AI generation.
- AI output is always candidate output: reviewable, source-grounded, rejectable, and never a direct substitute for user thinking.
