# Code of Conduct

MetaLearn OS is a learning-tool project. Contributions should be direct, respectful, and evidence-oriented.

## Expected Behavior

- Be precise and constructive.
- Keep claims about learning effects conservative.
- Respect privacy concerns around learning materials and self-assessments.
- Discuss tradeoffs with evidence and examples.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Publishing private user materials or credentials.
- Adding unsupported marketing claims.
- Pressuring maintainers to add unsafe AI behavior.
- Dismissing privacy or accessibility concerns without technical reasoning.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may close issues, edit comments, or block participation when behavior undermines the project's safety, privacy, or collaboration standards.
